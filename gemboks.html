GEMSBOKS

The gemsbok (Oryx gazella) is a species of large antelope native to the arid regions of southern Africa, including Namibia, Botswana, South Africa, and Angola.

It is known for its striking appearance, with long, straight horns and distinctive black and white facial markings.

The gemsbok is well adapted to desert environments and is capable of surviving in harsh conditions with limited water sources.

It is often found in small herds and is an important part of the ecosystem in its range.
