MONKEYS

A monkey is an agile, intelligent primate found in Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

live in trees, eat fruits, leaves, and insects, and are known for their social behavior and playful nature.

Monkeys come in various species, such as capuchins, spider monkeys, and macaques, but many face threats like habitat loss and hunting, prompting conservation efforts.
