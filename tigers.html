TIGERS

A tiger is a large carnivorous cat native to various parts of Asia, including India, Russia, and Southeast Asia.

is known for its striking orange coat with black stripes, which helps it camouflage in its forest habitat.

Tigers are apex predators and primarily hunt large ungulates such as deer and wild boar. They are solitary animals, with each individual maintaining a large territory to hunt and roam.

Tigers are iconic symbols of strength and beauty but are threatened by extinction, making their conservation a global priority.
